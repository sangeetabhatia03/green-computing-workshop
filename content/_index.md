# A (very) brief introduction to green computing practices

Writing and using software is an essential aspect of researchers' scientific practice. If you worry about climate change, and have ever wondered about the carbon footprint of your computing practices, then this workshop is for you. If you have never given a thought to the carbon footprint of your computing practices, then this workshop is *definitely* for you. Join us for an exciting day of talks and a training session that will give you plenty of food for thought as well as practical strategies for making your workflows more sustainable.
